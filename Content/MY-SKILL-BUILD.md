## Skill 1: caption-voiceover-writer

### Purpose
Transform content ideas into platform-specific captions and voiceover scripts optimized for maximum engagement across 9 major platforms.

### What It Does

**8-Step Workflow:**
1. Identify source (uploaded file, Google Doc, or one-off)
2. Select brand (OutAtlas or Travel in Pride)
3. Choose platforms (from 9 options)
4. Specify voiceover needs + tone
5. Review captions
6. Adjust as needed
7. Export to .docx
8. Optional: Add to Notion tasks

## Skill 2: video-storyboard

### Purpose
Act as professional Video Director, Storyboard Writer, and Shot List Producer. Build complete video storyboards from scratch with shot-by-shot sequencing, voiceover scripts, music guidance, and platform-specific captions.

### What It Does

**11-Step Workflow:**

1. **Intake Questions** — Gather platform, duration, topic, style, footage status, source
2. **Footage Inventory** — Describe what footage you have (if any)
3. **Shot List** — Generate detailed shots with priority levels (MUST HAVE / NICE TO HAVE / OPTIONAL)
4. **Voiceover Creation** — Write timestamped scripts with delivery notes (if voiceover-based)
5. **Storytelling Structure** — Build narrative arc with Act 1 / Act 2 / Act 3
6. **Shot-by-Shot Storyboard** — Format every shot with type, action, VO, text, music cues
7. **Music Guidance** — Provide vibe, tempo, genre, beat-sync moments, volume mixing
8. **Platform Captions** — Write optimized captions for each platform (6 platforms)
9. **Review & Adjust** — Allow approval, adjustments, or replacements
10. **Export as .docx** — Generate branded, professionally formatted storyboard
11. **Notion Integration** — Optional task creation for production workflow

### Video Types Supported

- YouTube Long-form (8–20+ min)
- YouTube Shorts (under 60 sec)
- Instagram Reels (15–90 sec)
- TikTok (15 sec – 10 min)
- Instagram Feed Video
- Cross-platform (multiple simultaneously)

### Key Features

- **Shot list generation** — MUST HAVE / NICE TO HAVE / OPTIONAL priorities
- **Voiceover scripts** — Timestamped with delivery notes and AI recommendations
- **Storytelling principles** — Chronological logic, hook-first, emotional pacing
- **Missing footage handling** — Creative workarounds and alternatives
- **Music briefs** — Vibe, tempo, beat-sync moments, volume mixing
- **6 platform captions** — TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Reddit
- **Brand voice** — OutAtlas + Travel in Pride built in
- **Printable shot lists** — On-set checkboxes for filming teams
- **Notion integration** — Optional production task tracking

### Trigger Phrases

```
"create a storyboard"
"storyboard this video"
"help me plan my video"
"build a shot list"
"I need a storyboard for my cruise video"
"plan my YouTube video"
"what shots do I need"
"help me edit this footage"
"storyboard my content calendar"
```

### Output

**Branded .docx file with:**
- Video Overview & Brief
- Shot List (if applicable)
- Voiceover Script (if applicable)
- Music Brief
- Shot-by-Shot Storyboard
- Missing Footage & Alternatives
- Platform Captions (6 platforms)
- Printable on-set shot list

### Storytelling Principles Built In

✅ Chronological default (unless intentionally non-chronological)
✅ Hook in first 6 seconds (mandatory)
✅ Establish before exploring (wide → medium → close-up)
✅ Emotion over logistics
✅ Quiet moments earn hype
✅ End on a promise
✅ One idea per shot
✅ Best shot goes second

### Platform Best Practices

**TikTok**
- 21–34 sec or 2–3 min sweet spots
- 9:16 vertical, full bleed
- Hook in first 1–2 seconds

**Instagram Reels**
- 15–90 sec
- 9:16 aspect ratio
- Hook in first 3 seconds

**YouTube Shorts**
- Under 60 sec strictly
- Full bleed vertical, no black bars
- Hook must work without audio

**YouTube Long-form**
- 30-sec hook rule
- Chapters at every major section
- Pattern interrupt every 60–90 sec

**Pinterest Video**
- 6–15 sec for awareness
- 2:3 or 9:16 vertical
- Text overlay required

**LinkedIn Video**
- 1–3 minutes
- Mandatory subtitles
- Square or landscape preferred
