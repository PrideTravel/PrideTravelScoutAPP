1. PLATFORM DIFFERENCES REQUIRE CAPTION REWRITING:

2. BRAND VOICE CONSISTENCY ACROSS ADAPTATION:
   All three captions maintain OutAtlas voice (personal, trusted, community-driven)
   while adapting to platform mechanics. Users should recognize the brand across
   all three.

   Rather than "Download now," all three CTAs position OutAtlas as a solution to
   a problem the user already feels (finding authentic queer-friendly travel spots,
   discovering vetted recommendations).

4. EACH PLATFORM PLAYS A ROLE IN FUNNEL:
   - Instagram: Top-of-funnel awareness (reach existing audience)
   - YouTube: Mid-funnel consideration (convert high-intent searchers)
   - Pinterest: Long-tail authority (sustain organic traffic + planning-stage users)

5. MEASUREMENT STRATEGY:
   - Instagram: Track saves, comments, link-in-bio clicks
   - YouTube: Track watch time, retention, click-through rate, description clicks
   - Pinterest: Track impressions, outbound clicks, re-pins
