## Overview

The **caption-voiceover-writer** skill is a professional social media content creation tool for OutAtlas and Travel in Pride. It transforms content ideas into platform-specific captions, hashtags, CTAs, and voiceover scripts optimized for maximum engagement across nine platforms.

### What It Does

- **8-step workflow** guides you from content idea to delivery-ready captions
- **Platform-specific formatting** for TikTok, Instagram (Reels/Feed/Stories), YouTube (Shorts/Long-form), Pinterest, LinkedIn, and Reddit
- **Voiceover script generation** with delivery notes and AI voiceover suggestions
- **.docx export** with branded formatting and professional structure
- **Brand voice consistency** across all platforms (OutAtlas + Travel in Pride)

### Who It's For

Content creators, marketing managers, and brand owners who need to:
- Write captions quickly across multiple platforms
- Maintain consistent brand voice
- Follow platform best practices
- Generate voiceover scripts for video content
- Manage urgent/one-off social posts

## How to Use

### Trigger Phrases

The skill activates when you say things like:

- "write captions for my posts"
- "caption these posts"
- "write voiceovers for my content"
- "I need captions for this week"
- "urgent post — write a caption"
- "something happened I need to post about"
- "write captions from my content calendar"

### Basic Workflow (8 Steps)

1. **Identify Source** — Upload file, paste Google Doc link, or describe one-off post
2. **Confirm Brand** — OutAtlas, Travel in Pride, or both
3. **Select Platforms** — TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Reddit
4. **Check Voiceover** — Do any posts need scripts? What tone?
5. **Write Captions** — Full platform-specific captions with hashtags and CTAs
6. **Review & Adjust** — Approve, tweak, or rewrite as needed
7. **Export to .docx** — Get a branded, formatted document
8. **Optional Notion Integration** — Add tasks to your dashboard

## Platform Best Practices Reference

### TikTok
- Hook in first line (no "hey guys")
- ~150 chars before truncation
- 3-5 hashtags max
- CTA: comments, follows, saves (no external links)
- Best times: Tue/Thu/Sat 7-9pm

### Instagram Reels
- Hook visible before 125-char truncation
- 8-15 hashtags
- Can include "link in bio"
- 2 optimal times: 11am-1pm or 7-9pm (Mon/Wed/Fri)

### YouTube Shorts
- Title: 60 chars max, keyword-focused
- Description: Primary keyword in first sentence
- 3 hashtags only (in description, not title)
- 5-8 tags for recommendations

### YouTube Long-Form
- Title: 60-70 chars, SEO-optimized
- Chapters with timestamps
- Keyword-rich description with links
- 8-12 tags

### Pinterest
- Title: 75-100 chars, keyword-rich
- Description: 150-300 chars, natural keywords
- No exclamation marks or emoji spam
- 5-10 hashtags
- Best days: Sat/Sun evenings

### LinkedIn
- Hook in first line
- ~210 chars before "see more"
- 3-5 professional hashtags
- Post as personal profile or brand page
- Best times: Tue-Thu 8-10am

### Reddit
- Zero self-promo tone
- Lead with genuine value
- Match subreddit culture
- No hashtags
- Mention brand only if natural

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does it take to get captions?**
A: One-off urgent posts take 5-10 minutes. Multi-post calendars take 15-20 minutes depending on platform count.

**Q: Can I use these captions as-is?**
A: Yes! All captions are ready to copy-paste. You can also request adjustments before export.

**Q: What if I need a different tone?**
A: Just specify in the voiceover request (Friendly, Hype, Informative, Bold, Newsy, or Calm).

**Q: Do you support other platforms?**
A: Currently covers TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, LinkedIn, and Reddit. Contact support for other platforms.

**Q: Can I export to other formats?**
A: Currently exports to .docx. PDF export coming soon.

**Q: What about hashtag research?**
A: The skill uses established best practices for hashtag counts and types per platform. No real-time trending research.
