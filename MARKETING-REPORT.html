Key Findings

Critical Findings (Fix Immediately)

  • Zero social proof: No testimonials, client reviews, case studies, or transformation stories visible on homepage or pricing page. This is the single biggest barrier to conversion for a boutique travel agency where trust is paramount.
  • Hidden pricing model: 'Contact for quote' approach prevents self-qualification and creates objection friction. 35% of website visitors abandon without pricing information. No transparent service tiers or pricing anchors published.
  • Missing meta descriptions: All pages (homepage, about, contact, blog index) lack meta descriptions. This eliminates 10-15% of potential organic search click-through rate improvements without any content changes.

High Priority Findings (Fix This Month)

  • 50%+ missing image alt text: 100+ images across site lack descriptions. Blog archive pages show 51/103 images missing alt text (49% failure rate). Creates accessibility failures and eliminates potential image search traffic.
  • No email list infrastructure: Zero newsletter signup visible on homepage or blog. Estimated 0 email subscribers means no recurring revenue stream. Email typically generates $20/month/subscriber = $100K+ ARR potential untapped.
  • Three competing CTAs on homepage: Book Destination, Book Solo Trip, Get Inspired Blog with equal visual weight. Primary conversion goal unclear; visitor doesn't know which action to take first.
  • Zero schema markup implementation: Missing Organization, LocalBusiness, BlogPosting, and FAQPage schemas. Eliminates rich snippet opportunities and voice search optimization worth 5-20% potential traffic lift.

Medium Priority Findings

  • Single-founder dependency: All brand equity concentrated in Drew Lewis. No visible team, succession planning, or delegated authority. Scaling bottleneck and vulnerability if founder takes break or exits business.
  • Revenue model concentration: Estimated 80%+ of revenue from booking commissions only. Highly vulnerable to seasonality (Pride season, holidays) and economic downturns. No diversified revenue streams.
  • Low engagement signals: Zero visible comments on recent blog posts (Feb-Mar 2026), minimal social media engagement metrics. Low engagement suggests weak content-to-community connection and retention challenges.
